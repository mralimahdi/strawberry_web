About Our Farm

Located in the prestigious ARID Agriculture University, our strawberry farm combines traditional farming wisdom with cutting-edge agricultural technology. We're committed to delivering the freshest, sweetest strawberries while maintaining sustainable farming practices.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide our community with the highest quality strawberries while promoting sustainable agriculture. Today, we're proud to be one of the leading strawberry producers in the region.
